C) How To Edit PSD File - top

In order to edit the file, you need Adobe Photoshop installed on your system, and the fonts mentioned in the credits installed.
As you can see on the bottom, the layers are well organized and groped into folders, which make editing very easy.
So, all you have to do is select the layer from the bottom, and then edit it with Adobe Photoshop.

How can I see the hover in PSD?

You can see the effects by clicking on the eyeball in the layer you want to see.Effects layers invisible at startup. Each psd has different effects, you will have to click on your eyeball to see them

That's all! Enjoy.
